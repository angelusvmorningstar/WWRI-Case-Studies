# M2 — Options Development

## M2.5 Internal — Option Development (Potential Solutions)

### Problem Statements
- What are their stated challenges?
- Apply the **5 Whys** to identify the 1 to 3 fundamental challenges

### Develop Options
- Using Whitewater **P1-P4 processes**, apply to the core problem area and explore options
- Think through how these would solve the fundamental challenges

### Presentation QC
- Develop options and initial costings
- Create a **3 to 7 slide deck**
- Get QC on the presentation

## M2.0 External — 2nd Meeting with Client to Co-create Solutions

### Before the Meeting
- Check the fundamental analysis but **NOT the solutions** before the meeting

### The M2 Presentation
- Arrange a **90-minute boardroom meeting with a whiteboard**
- Share feedback on problems/key challenges (avoid PowerPoint if possible)
- Restate challenges and why move to the fundamental challenge
- Present options informally through their eyes, co-create on the whiteboard

### Packaging the Solutions
- Using the **client's language**, position solutions into their broader area of responsibility
- Ask how they will take this forward, who will be involved, and how Whitewater can help with their internal approval journey

## Self-Check
**Is this solution ready for M3 presentation to the Full Exec Team?**
- Roles and decision-making authority — any Whitewater meetings needed?
- Influencers to get support/approval
- Explain costing and Whitewater gaining new savings **3 to 5x our fees**
