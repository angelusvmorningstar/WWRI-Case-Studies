# M1 — 1st Formal Meeting: Exploring Dreams & Challenges

## Overview
This is your first formal meeting with the prospect. The goal is to **listen and explore**, not to present solutions.

## Key Principles
- **See the world through the leader's eyes.** Every client has problems — identify the deeper intractable challenges.
- **Be genuinely curious.** Ask business-relevant questions. Listen. **Do NOT add solutions.**
- **Share insights being inquisitive.** Explore their dream and challenges.

## During the Meeting
1. Determine if this person is responsible and empowered to address the challenges
2. Is this person accountable for the needed transformation? If not, ask for an introduction to the transformation leader
3. Play back the **2 to 3 challenges** where Whitewater can add most value

## After the Meeting
If pursuing, set up the next meeting:
- "I work with a group of experts (ex-CEOs) that have solved these problems, let me see if they are interested in sharing their experience... and arrange a follow-up discussion."
- Agree next meeting in **10 days to 2 weeks**

## Self-Check
**Is this person transformational and interested?**
- Did they engage genuinely with the challenges discussed?
- Are they accountable for driving transformation?
- Is there real momentum, or was it polite but non-committal?

If No — **Stop. Do not pursue.**
