# M0 — Identifying Potential Leaders & Organisation Clients

## Overview
This is the casual engagement stage. You're identifying whether a prospect is transformational and worth pursuing.

## Steps

### M0.1 — Overview of M+ Process
Familiarise yourself with the M+ Process before engaging with prospects.

### M0.2 — Getting Ready to Engage
Work with your Mentor to identify and research the client profile:
- Industry, strategy, financials, structure, market
- LinkedIn posting and public presence
- Potential areas of interest
- Identify the Transformational Leaders
- Ask yourself: "Why do they want to hear from me?"

### M0.3 — Client Contact
**If the client is known:**
- They may not be aware of Whitewater
- Arrange an informal casual chat

**If the client isn't known:**
- Is an introduction possible?
- Another contact method may be required

**For known contacts:** Have a casual chat to understand their challenges at a high level.

**For unknown contacts:** Establish casual contact via chat, LinkedIn, email, etc.

## Self-Check
**Is this prospect transformational and interested?**
- Are they facing genuine transformation challenges?
- Do they have the appetite and authority for change?
- Is there a real opportunity here, or are you forcing it?

If No — **Stop. Do not pursue.** Your time is better spent elsewhere.
